About Me

I am a second-year PhD student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, specializing in Environmental Science and Sustainable Development.

My research focuses on:

  • Sustainable land use planning
  • Resilience in rural and urban social-ecological systems
  • Ecosystem service modeling and evaluation

I use an interdisciplinary and pragmatic approach to tackle real-world sustainability challenges.
