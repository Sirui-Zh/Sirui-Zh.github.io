Research Projects

1. Urban Land Use Planning for Human Well-being

Designing equitable and efficient urban planning models that optimize land use to promote multidimensional well-being while maintaining ecological sustainability.

2. Resilience in Rural Social-Ecological Systems

A systematic review and meta-analysis of global rural resilience indicators, focusing on the integration of environmental, economic, and social dimensions.

3. Protected Area Boundary Optimization

Using ecosystem service flow modeling and stakeholder analysis to inform sustainable national park management and spatial boundary adjustments.

4. Grain Production Dynamics in China

Analyzed 20 years of national agricultural data to explore socio-economic and natural drivers of grain output, with policy recommendations for sustainable agriculture.
